Frequently Asked Questions

How can I trust my items are returned in working condition?

We evaluate all items that are listed with WWHO, and if the item is returned damaged, are able to charge the borrower the amount of the item.

How do you calculate fees?

We collect 20% of the amount an item is rented out for.

What kinds of payment options do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, as well as paypal.

When do you expect to launch?

We expect to launch by the year 2020.
